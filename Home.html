Welcome to the UC Irvine chapter of the National Organization for Rare Disorders, or NORD at UCI! We are a student-run organization advocating for individuals with rare diseases and supporting the institutions that serve them. We also work with physicians and researchers to find cures for rare diseases. 

Founded in Fall 2020, we are proud to be the first official branch of NORD at a University of California school!
